Diary itself has a really simple layout. I will probably add some UI to make it more appealing visually, but that’s not the focus so far. Each entrance displays the heading and the date it was posted - different color for each month.
Search function available.
When created and submitted a diary entrance can’t no longer be edited or deleted from the app. Can, however, be exported. A diary is a historic document and one shouldn’t rewrite history... so no deleting or editing here . The only way to delete it is to delete the account itself.
